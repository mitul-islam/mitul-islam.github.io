About Me

I am a postdoc at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in the Sciences (Leipzig) in the group led by Anna Wienhard.

Prior to this, I was a postdoc at Heidelberg University (Research Station Geometry and Dynamics) with Beatrice Pozzetti. I did my PhD at the University of Michigan (2016-2021) with Ralf Spatzier.
Before that, I was a student at the Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata (M.Math) and Jadavpur University (undergraduate).
