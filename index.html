About Me

I am a tenure-track Reader (that is, Assistant Professor) at the Tata Institute of Fundamental Research (TIFR) in Mumbai. I started at TIFR in August 2025.

Previously, I have held postdoctoral positions at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in the Sciences (MPI-MiS) (in Wienhard's group) and Heidelberg University (in Pozzetti's group).
I did my PhD at the University of Michigan with Ralf Spatzier. Before that, I studied at the Indian Statistical Institute and Jadavpur University in Kolkata, my hometown.
