Research Interests

I am broadly interested in:

  • Geometric structures on manifolds, their moduli spaces, and group actions preserving geometric structures,
  • Discrete subgroups of Lie groups,
  • Non-positive curvature phenomena and geometric group theory,
  • Local and global rigidity of group actions on compact manifolds.
